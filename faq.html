Aim Training:
How can I improve my aim?
You have to commit to practicing, a mix of both in game and aim trainer practice will help you get the aim that you want to achieve, but it's not easy. The main thing is to ensure that your practice is highly focussed, and you're training scenarios and aim styles that you know you need to improve at and not just scenarios you enjoy playing.
Is aimlabs or kovaaks better?
kovaaks = more customizable but more complex
aim lab = less customizable but more helpful to learning
(use kovaaks if you know what to practice and aimlabs if you don't)
